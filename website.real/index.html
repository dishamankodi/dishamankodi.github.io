Past Projects

During my internship at Potentiometric Probes, I designed an imaging system that minimized the cost of fluorescence imaging. After conducting many experiments to troubleshoot and compare the mean signal and background voltages of different detection systems, I synchroinzed a low-cost camera and single pixel detector to optimize resolution and speed of the produced image.

In Gyor, Hungary, I collaborated with Hungarian students during the Entrepreneurship Leadership Academy to develop a prototype and business model for NicoTeam, a nicotine monitoring toothbrush. This was an awesome exerpeince because it opened my eyes to global healthcare and entrepreneurship, and it let me travel around Hungary!

Current Projects

I am working on my senior design project, which involves creating a human study inducing cognitive load on subjects in order to ultimately correlate facial and vocal patterns with levels of cognitive load. This project is sponsored by the Navy, and I am excited to analyze our results next semester.
